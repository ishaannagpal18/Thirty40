Welcome to Thirty40

We have first hand experience in what is required to become a tennis professional. We have built a community where we have all the resources to help a player by providing him counselling as per his/her goal.
If you are looking for anything in tennis, Thirty40 is your one stop shop. We have the largest network of coaches worldwide who can train you to become the best version of yourself.... We provide cost-effective ways for players to train worldwide and play international tournaments by initially training them at our sports academies in India and then send them abroad on scholarships and arrange sponsorships.
They are sent abroad either as:
- Professional athletes at a tennis academy.
- Student-Athletes to play and study simultaneously in Universities, or
- Players to represent prestigious clubs in league matches.
