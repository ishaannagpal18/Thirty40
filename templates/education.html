Welcome to Thirty40

We help students find the courses and institutions that are exceptionally well positioned for them. Connecting students with the right course in the right university or institution and the right country is our forte.
We specialise in facilitating admissions in public and community universities worldwide. We provide transparency and true information to place students in the best universities in Europe for FREE or for as low as INR 15,000... We deal with more than 500 top-notch schools and colleges across the world.
Most Indians are not able to avail opportunities to study abroad because of the expenses and tuition fee as they don't know about the possible scholarships. Our goal is to empower students by making them aware about the possibilities of studying in the world's best universities for free and to add to that, be paid to study. By doing this, we want to ensure that everyone has access to quality education and their finances don't put a stop to their dreams of flying high and excelling in their careers.
